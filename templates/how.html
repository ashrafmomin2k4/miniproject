Our Detection Technology

Static APK Analysis

The tool uses the Androguard library to safely deconstruct and read the contents of the APK file. It extracts crucial metadata like package name, version, and, most importantly, the permissions it requires to run. This is done without executing the app, so there is zero risk.

Rule-Based Permission Scanning

Malware often exploits specific permissions. We look for the following dangerous permissions:

  • SEND_SMS — Can send texts without your knowledge
  • READ_CONTACTS — Can steal your contact list
  • RECORD_AUDIO — Can listen through your microphone
  • CAMERA — Can access your camera

Classification: The tool uses a rule-based engine to classify the APK as Clean, Suspicious, or Malicious based on the number and combination of these risky permissions.

Hash-Based Blacklist Detection

We compute a unique SHA256 hash for every uploaded APK. This digital fingerprint is compared against a database of known malicious applications. If there's a match, the app is immediately flagged.
