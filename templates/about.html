About APK Malware Scanner

Our Mission

Mobile applications have become an integral part of everyday life. With this growth comes an increase in malware disguised as legitimate applications. Our mission is to provide a simple, powerful tool that empowers users to identify these threats before they can cause harm, protecting personal data from being stolen or exploited.

The Technology

This project is built in Python and leverages the Androguard library for robust static analysis. It's designed to be a lightweight and efficient first line of defense against mobile threats.

The Future

We are constantly working to improve our detection capabilities. Our next step is to integrate machine learning classifiers trained on thousands of app features to more accurately identify new and evolving malware threats.
